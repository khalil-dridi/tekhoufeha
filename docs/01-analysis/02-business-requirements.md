# Business Requirements

---

# 1. Business Context

The rapid growth of online marketplaces has made buying and selling second-hand products easier than ever. However, existing platforms often rely on unstructured conversations, making negotiations time-consuming and inefficient.

TekhouFeha aims to improve this experience by providing a structured marketplace where product offers, seller decisions, and buyer interactions follow a clear and organized workflow.

---

# 2. Business Opportunity

Second-hand commerce continues to grow as more people seek affordable products and sustainable consumption.

TekhouFeha introduces a different buying experience by allowing sellers to choose between two selling modes:

- Fixed Price
- Make an Offer

This approach provides greater flexibility while reducing unnecessary negotiations and improving transaction efficiency.

---

# 3. Business Objectives

The platform aims to:

- Simplify second-hand buying and selling.
- Improve communication between buyers and sellers.
- Reduce unnecessary conversations.
- Encourage serious purchase offers.
- Create a transparent offer management process.
- Build user trust.
- Deliver a scalable marketplace for future growth.

---

# 4. Business Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Responsibilities |
|-------------|------------------|
| Visitors | Browse and search products |
| Users | Publish products, submit offers, manage transactions |
| Administrators | Moderate products, manage users, handle reports |
| Platform Owner | Define business strategy and platform growth |

---

# 5. Business Needs

The platform must satisfy the following business needs:

## User Management

- Allow users to register.
- Allow users to authenticate.
- Allow users to manage their profiles.

---

## Product Management

- Allow users to publish products.
- Allow users to edit their products.
- Allow users to remove products.
- Allow visitors to browse products.
- Allow product search and filtering.

---

## Selling Modes

The platform shall support two selling modes:

### Fixed Price

Products are published with a predefined selling price.

### Make an Offer

Products are published without displaying a selling price.

Interested users submit confidential purchase offers.

---

## Offer Management

The platform shall:

- Allow users to submit offers.
- Allow sellers to review received offers.
- Allow sellers to accept, reject, or keep offers pending.
- Reserve a product after an accepted offer.

---

## Communication

The platform shall create a private conversation only after a seller accepts an offer.

---

## Notifications

The platform shall notify users about:

- New offers.
- Accepted offers.
- Rejected offers.
- New chat messages.

---

## Administration

Administrators shall be able to:

- Manage users.
- Moderate products.
- Review reports.
- Remove inappropriate content.

---

# 6. Business Constraints

The MVP includes the following constraints:

- Web application only.
- No online payment.
- No delivery management.
- No AI price estimation.
- No recommendation system.
- No mobile application.

---

# 7. Assumptions

The project assumes that:

- Users have Internet access.
- Users act in good faith.
- Users provide accurate product information.
- The platform acts only as an intermediary between buyers and sellers.

---

# 8. Business Risks

Potential risks include:

- Fake product listings.
- Fraudulent users.
- Low platform adoption.
- Spam offers.
- Security threats.
- Competition from existing marketplaces.

---

# 9. Success Criteria

The platform will be considered successful if:

- Users actively publish products.
- Users submit purchase offers.
- Sellers successfully complete transactions.
- Users remain active on the platform.
- Customer satisfaction is continuously improved.
